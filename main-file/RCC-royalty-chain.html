Royalty Chain Features


Master

● Register your songs in the global database - multi-territorial multi-rights core database for music works, master recordings, record releases.

MEMBERS MANAGER

● Keep your members’ and/or partners’ data up to date
● End-to-end members registration and data management app
● Get your members to feel privileged by providing them with at-source information automatically, making your communication convenient and smooth

Tracer

● Track the usage of your songs on DSPs across the globe
● Process zillions of lines of DSP usage reports to identify your songs and claim your rights directly
● The tracer utilizes the entire operation workflow with DSR, UCR and other types of reports, CCID and CCR claiming and invoicing procedures

ROYALTY DISTRIBUTOR

● Manage various types and formats of reports
● Match and disburse funds to appropriate parties
● Provide detailed statements to your partners
